Popular drink


Thnaot coffee is made from the purest fruit in Cambodia during the dry season.

New drink


Coffee Milo: A rich and comforting blend of smooth coffee and creamy Milo, perfectly mixed to create a delicious balance of bold coffee flavor and chocolate malt sweetness. Served hot or iced, this energizing drink is a satisfying choice for coffee lovers looking for a unique twist on a classic favorite.

Dessert


Num thnaot: is a traditional Cambodian dessert made with natural palm fruit, giving it a sweet, fragrant flavor. Its soft, fluffy texture and rich aroma make it a beloved treat, perfect to enjoy with a cup of coffee.

Seasonal Drink

Summer drink


Rambutan coffee: A unique fusion of bold coffee and sweet, juicy rambutan. The tropical fruit's delicate flavor adds a refreshing twist to the rich aroma of coffee, creating a smooth and memorable drink.

Fall drink


Guava coffee: A refreshing blend of freshly brewed coffee and fragrant guava. The fruit's natural sweetness and subtle tartness complement the deep coffee flavor, resulting in a balanced and exotic beverage.

Winter drink


Star coffee: A vibrant combination of rich coffee and crisp star fruit. Its light citrus notes bring a refreshing brightness to every sip, creating a smooth drink with a uniquely tropical character.

Spring drink


Lychee coffee: An elegant mix of aromatic coffee and sweet lychee. The floral and juicy notes of lychee soften the boldness of the coffee, delivering a refreshing and sophisticated flavor experience.
