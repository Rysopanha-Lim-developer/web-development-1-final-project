About Us

More than coffee.
A place to be.

Our mission is to provide Cambodians with quality coffee at an affordable price, while creating a space that welcomes everyone like home.

Our Story

Started in a small classroom and a very big dream.

Tnaot Café began as a converted corner shop in 2022, founded by six friends who believed that everyone deserved great coffee with a great amostphere. Just quality beans, honest craft, and a corner of the city that feels entirely your own.

Location

Five locations across Phnom Penh.

Find us across the city, each branch designed to feel like a neighbourhood home.

What we stand for

The things we never compromise on.

Ethically sourced

Every bean we use is local. We pay fair prices and visit our partners annually.

Craft over convenience

There are no shortcuts in our kitchen, only deliberate choices.

Community first

We include everyone and make sure everyon is comfortable. This place belongs to the neighborhood.
